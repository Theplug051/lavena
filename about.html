About Our History

Lavéna Florist was founded by Vilma, a skilled florist and entrepreneur dedicated to empowering women and uplifting her community through business.

We are a modern online florist offering fresh, handcrafted arrangements for all occasions. Every bouquet is made to order using quality flowers and clean, elegant design. We focus on convenience, creativity, and reliable delivery Nation wide.

Blooms of Kindness Initiative

Once a month, we deliver flower arrangements and small food parcels to families, elders, or community members who need a little extra love. It’s our way of giving back to the same community that supports our business, reminding people that they are valued and not forgotten.

Our Blooming Values

  • Reliability Matters

Get to know the Team
