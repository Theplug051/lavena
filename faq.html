Help & Support

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

Find everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and flower care below.

How do I place an order? +
You can browse our flower collections, select your preferred bouquet or arrangement, and add it to your cart. Once you’re ready, proceed to checkout and follow the secure payment steps.
Do you offer same-day delivery? +
Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 11AM. Orders placed after that time will be delivered the following day.
Can I customize my bouquet? +
Yes! If you’d like a custom arrangement, please contact us directly via WhatsApp or our contact form, and we’ll be happy to create something unique for your occasion.
How do I care for my flowers once they arrive? +

To keep your blooms fresh for longer:

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing in water.
  • Change water every 2 days.
  • Keep flowers away from direct sunlight, heat, and fruit.
  • Add the provided flower food for longer freshness.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations

Advance orders can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the delivery date for a full refund. Same-day orders cannot be cancelled once confirmed.

Refunds

Refunds are offered if flowers arrive damaged or the wrong item is delivered. Please contact us within 24 hours with photos.
